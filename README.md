AGRICONNECT – A DIGITAL BRIDGE BETWEEN FARMERS AND AGRICULTURAL EXPERTS

OVERVIEW:
AgriConnect is a digital platform that connects farmers with agricultural experts for real-time, localized guidance and personalized support.
This repository showcases the Figma UI design built for clarity, simplicity, and accessibility.

PROBLEM & SOLUTION:
Farmers face challenges such as lack of expert advice, difficulty identifying crop diseases, and limited access to market or scheme updates.
AgriConnect addresses these issues by enabling direct communication between farmers and experts through a simple and user-friendly interface.

UI DESIGN FEATURES:
Intuitive navigation and clean layout.
Sections for Discussions, Weather, Market Prices, and Government Schemes.
Focused on usability and accessibility for rural users.

TOOLS USED:
Figma – for UI design.
